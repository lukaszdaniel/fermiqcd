Detailed Description

a field of vectors of complex numbers

Example: 

///    int box[]={10,10,10};
///    mdp_lattice lattice(3,box);
///    mdp_vector_field h(lattice,10);
///    mdp_site x(lattice);
///    forallsites(x)
///      h(x)=0.0+0.0*I;
///
